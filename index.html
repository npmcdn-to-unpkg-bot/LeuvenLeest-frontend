LeuvenLeest WebApp

A project by #oSoc16 and CultuurConnect

What is LeuvenLeest?

LeuvenLeest is an app focused on finding and sharing cool reading places with 3 distincts dimensions to it:

Where?

First of all, it helps you discover cool reading spots and filter them to your likings. Maybe you'd like a quiet outside place, or somewhere with chill music and good coffee? LeuvenLeest will help you find your perfect spot
If you find a place you like, you can check-in that location, or add it to your favourite spots, and it will be easier for you to find it again later.
And if you find a place that's not listed in LeuvenLeest, you can even add it to the app!

Who?

You also have the possibillity to add people to your friends, so you can easily join them for nice reading sessions together.
Maybe you have a friend who always finds the best spots around Leuven and you'd be interested in finding some of those ?

What?

You can also let people know what kind of books you are reading.
Who knows, you might find out that there's a lot of people interested in the same books as you in your neighborhood and you'll be able to set up a local book club ?
Letting you search by specific book genre will help you surround yourself with people that share your interests.

LeuvenLeest is a great tool to bring together people that share a love for reading!

What is oSoc?

Open Summer of code, or #oSoc, is a 4 week programme in different Belgian cities during the summer and is part of Open Knowledge Belgium.
The goal: provide Belgian based students the training, network and support necessary to transform open innovation projects into powerful real-world services.

What is CultuurConnect?

Cultuurconnect is a labo where libraries, cultural and community centres try to find solutions for digital challenges in the local cultural strategy.
